Frequently Asked Questions

Do you have a question? Well, we've got answers

1. Who are we?

Carizma is a US based company on a mission to make car selling easy.

Behind the scenes, we employ people who care about providing customers a stress-free car selling experience.

2. How does instant offer work?

After answering a handful of questions about your car, we provide you with an instant offer, no haggling or sleazy salesman included! This offer is based on market value and information provided by you. Once you receive the offer, you have 7 days to accept. No pressure!

3. Are you in my State?

Most likely, yes! We partner with transporters in all 50 states to pick up and move cars right where we need them.

4. Do I need to clean my car before it’s picked up?

No scrubbing required, but we do recommend removing all personal items. It doesn’t hurt to sift through any trash to make sure nothing of value is left behind as we cannot guarantee that anything that is towed away will be searched for or returned. Don’t forget to remove your plates.

5. What do you do with my car?

It depends on a few things, but most of the time we resell them. Sometimes cars can be scraped if they are in rough shape. Most importantly, we get them out of your driveway!
